About Me

Just a kid who codes

Hey, I'm Kigan 👋

I'm a 14-year-old 9th grader at Westview High School in Phoenix, AZ. I picked up coding about two years ago when I got curious about how websites actually work — so I opened YouTube, found a HTML tutorial, and never really stopped.
